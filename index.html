2015 PostGIS day is here!

It's the third Thursday of November, and as every year, also a day to celebrate our favourite spatial database. From the CartoDB blog:

Like GIS Day, PostGIS day is a grassroots effort open to everyone and is a wonderful opportunity to let people know about mapping, spatial operations, and discuss with friends the deep impact that maps, digital or otherwise, have had on our global culture and society.

Events

There are some events on this day to promote PostGIS. Do you know any other, share it with us! If you are lucky enough to be near of any of them, don't miss the opportunity to attend and meet other SQL-lovers.
